🗺️ Phase-wise Study Roadmap
Exam tak ka poora journey — Phase by phase plan karo
1

Phase 1 — Foundation Building

Month 1–2 · 60 Days
  • Sabse pehle apna syllabus print karo aur wall par lagao
  • Aashish Sir se Number System, % , Ratio se shuru karo
  • Ankush Sir se Puzzles basics + Syllogism shuru karo
  • Nimisha Ma'am se RC techniques + Grammar rules
  • Dr. Gaurav Garg se Banking System basics + RBI
  • Khush Sir ka daily CA — roz 30 min minimum
  • Notes banana ZARURI hai — baar baar padho
2

Phase 2 — Concept Strengthening

Month 3–4 · 60 Days
  • DI (Data Interpretation) — Aashish Sir ke saath deep dive
  • Advanced Puzzles + Machine Input-Output — Ankush Sir
  • Para Jumbles + Cloze Test + Vocab building
  • Government schemes + RBI policy deep study
  • Weekly Mini Mocks shuru karo (section-wise)
  • Previous year questions solve karna shuru karo
  • Weak topics identify karo aur double time do
3

Phase 3 — Speed & Accuracy

Month 5–6 · 60 Days
  • Full Mock Test roz — analyze karo, improve karo
  • Math mein shortcut + mental math practice
  • Reasoning mein time-bound puzzle solving
  • English mein ek passage daily compulsory
  • Error log maintain karo — same galti dobaara nahi
  • Descriptive writing practice shuru karo (Mains ke liye)
  • Static GK + Banking terms rapid revision
4

Phase 4 — Mains Preparation

Month 7–8 · After Prelims
  • Prelims ke baad ek din rest phir mains schedule shuru
  • Advanced DI + High-level reasoning focus
  • Essay + Letter writing (Descriptive Paper)
  • Economy + Current Affairs — in-depth coverage
  • Mains-level mock tests — full 3 hour attempts
  • Interview preparation (PO ke liye) — begin basic
5

Phase 5 — Final Revision & Interview

Last 30 Days + Interview Prep
  • Poora notes ka rapid revision — ek baar complete sweep
  • Last 5 years ke cut-off dekho — target set karo
  • Mock interview practice — confident speaking
  • Banking current affairs last 6 months
  • Mental fitness — meditation + positive mindset
  • Confidence building — tum tayyar ho! 💪
⚡ Smart Strategy Tips
Topper nahi, smart workers banate hain rank

🎯 Mock Test Strategy

  • Attempt order: English → Reasoning → Math (save tough for last)
  • Reasoning mein pehle easy topics karo, phir puzzles
  • Har mock ke baad 45 min analysis compulsory
  • Score nahi, accuracy improve karo pehle
  • Week mein 5 mocks minimum — test series lo

📒 Notes & Revision Strategy

  • Ek subject ke liye ek notebook — clean notes
  • Formulas + Shortcuts ek alag sheet par
  • Revision 1: Same day evening (5 min recap)
  • Revision 2: Weekly once
  • Revision 3: Monthly full notes read

💡 Weak Subject Rescue Plan

  • Math mein weak? → Roz 2 topics YouTube se, 25 qs practice
  • English mein weak? → Hindu editorial roz padhna shuru karo
  • Reasoning slow? → Timer lagao — har puzzle mein
  • Banking weak? → Flashcards banao — terms yaad karo
  • Weak ko ignore karo toh cut-off tak nahi pahuncho

🏆 Exam Hall Strategy

  • Pehle 2 min — poora paper scan karo
  • Easy se shuru karo — confidence build hoga
  • Ek question par 2 min se zyada mat ruko — skip karo
  • Guess mat karo jab tak 60% confident na ho
  • Negative marking ke saath accuracy hi asli scoring hai
